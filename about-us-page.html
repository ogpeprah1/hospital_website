OUR STORY

Nestled in the heart of KNUST campus, GreatMinds Health Care stands as a beacon of healing for the community. Founded over a century by the visionary Dr. Obiri Yeboah, the hospital has grown from humble beginning to become a renowned healthcare institution. From emergency care to specialized treatments, GreatMinds Health Care offers a comprehensive range of services to meet the diverse needs of its patients.
